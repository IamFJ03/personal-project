Our Story

Established in 2000, we manufacture high-quality MS Wire Products, including coated shelves and baskets for commercial refrigeration. With facilities in Kalyan and Silvasa, we combine innovation and precision to deliver reliable, industry-focused solutions. Our strong R&D and quality control ensure excellence, earning us a trusted reputation for over two decades.

Our Products

Sheen Industries specializes in high-quality wire storage solutions designed for commercial use. Our range includes Round and Stripe Wire Coated Shelves, offering durability and optimized display space. We also provide Wire Coated Baskets, including Plain, Pocket (with and without mesh), ensuring easy access and secure storage. Additionally, our Back Grill enhances safety and ventilation in high-heat environments. Crafted from mild steel and coated with white LDPE, our products deliver exceptional performance, reliability, and value for dynamic commercial spaces.
